## Quick Implementation Guide

1. **Download images** from Unsplash/Pexels using the search terms above
2. **Save images** to `./images/` folder with descriptive names:
   - `outcomes-dashboard.jpg`
   - `our-approach-consulting.jpg`
   - `ai-partners-technology.jpg`
   - `about-founder.jpg`
3. **Update HTML** - Replace the `<div class="image-placeholder">` sections with:
   ```html
   <img src="./images/your-image-name.jpg" alt="Description" />
   ```

## Image Specifications
- **Recommended size**: 1200x800px or larger (will be scaled down)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:10 or 3:2 works best
- **Style**: Professional, modern, dark/tech aesthetic to match your site
